Climate Change&Sustainability Project:

SOLAR BELGRADE

Database & map of existing solar power plant locations in Belgrade, Serbia

Within: Climate-KIC Pioneers Into Practice Mobility Programme
Author: Jolanta Pilch
Hosting company: Global Sustainability Experts

Description: Within the Solar Belgrade project (October 2018, Belgrade, Serbia) I was working on the topic related to solar energy market in Serbia. From the social point of view I was applying System Innovation tools to define the status quo of solar energy sector and to identify its main challenges. The technical part of my project focused on mapping the potential locations for the new solar PV installations (for business opportunities) as well as existing solar power plants in Belgrade. The result of the latter, a simple GIS map application, can be found below and is aimed to promote solar energy initiatives undertaken so far in Belgrade.
